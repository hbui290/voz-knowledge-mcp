# Insight Writing For VOZ Threads

Use this reference when turning archived VOZ threads into reusable knowledge, experience notes, or natural-language guides.

## Goal

Write like a person who read the full thread and is explaining the useful field experience to another person. The output should feel like hard-won notes, not a generic SEO article or a mechanical list of posts.

## Source Discipline

- Base claims on the archived posts and inspected links.
- Distinguish clearly between:
  - what the source says directly,
  - what multiple posts imply,
  - what you infer cautiously.
- Do not invent details for dynamic links that cannot be inspected.
- Preserve important links near the insight they support.
- For screenshots/images, cite the image link next to the relevant source post or topic; do not over-interpret images without visible text or surrounding post context.

## Link Handling

Remove only link noise:

- `voz.vn/goto/post?id=...`
- profile/account/share/quote links,
- app store signatures from forum clients,
- smilies, emoji CDN, avatars, `data:image` placeholders.

Keep and inspect meaningful links:

- VOZ thread/post links,
- VOZ attachments,
- YouTube channels/videos,
- Reddit threads,
- Facebook/Reels,
- TikTok,
- X/Twitter,
- Telegram/tool/file/resource links.

## Writing Shape

Prefer sections like:

- "Nguoi moi hay hoi sai..."
- "Bay nam o dau..."
- "Dung cai nay nhu the nao..."
- "Source dang noi gi..."
- "Ap dung thuc te..."

Each major insight should include:

1. The plain-language takeaway.
2. Why it matters.
3. What to do or avoid.
4. The relevant source links or attachment links.

## Avoid

- Generic advice with no source-specific meaning: "hay kien tri", "hay lam content chat luong", "can toi uu".
- Dumping many raw posts as proof instead of synthesizing.
- A dead appendix of links that is not connected to the text.
- Deleting useful attachments/external links while cleaning forum noise.
- Treating a screenshot as a full claim when the surrounding post only uses it as context.

## Good Style

Use natural Vietnamese. It can be direct and practical:

- "Bật kiếm tiền không phải cái đích; nó chỉ là cửa thu ngân. Nếu không có người xem thật thì cửa đó mở cũng vô nghĩa."
- "AI nên nằm sau hậu trường: research, outline, tool edit. Nếu AI nằm hết trên mặt tiền, kênh trông như content farm."
- "View US/EU ngon hơn, nhưng không miễn phí: có thuế, setup, ngôn ngữ, tệp và rủi ro vận hành."

## Final Check

Before finalizing a guide, ask:

- Did I cover every major repeated topic in the thread?
- Did I inspect content links, not just list them?
- Did I preserve attachment links without over-reading screenshots?
- Is this useful to a reader who wants to act, or is it just a summary?
- Are there any source limitations I should state plainly?
